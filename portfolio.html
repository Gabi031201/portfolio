My name is Gabriella Mayer, and I am a second-year graphic design student at the University of Sopron. With this portfolio, I aim to showcase what I have learned and the work I have created during these years. The portfolio includes typography and branding design projects that reflect my personal creative style.

Menu Redesign

In this typography task, we were required to recreate an existing menu. Something simpler, more easily understandable, and typographically well-crafted.

Umbrella Branding

In this task, we need to design an umbrella branding concept for TAEG. The logo consists of three elements, each of which is independently functional. It reflects a forestry-inspired atmosphere.

Logo Design

Our task was to design a logo accompanied by suitable typography. Alongside the logo, we had to create a fictional branding concept that effectively reflects the visual style of the symbol and connects seamlessly with it.
